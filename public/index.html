7 primitives live · For agents · Why
Data designed · Primary source · No other incentives

Public data,

made readable.

AI is a useful tool. But it's trained to sound confident — not to show its work. Search is useful too. But it optimizes for clicks. Fewer people are reading the primary record. More are outsourcing judgment to interfaces that weren't built for that.

These were. Data designed — direct interfaces to the federal record, built with one purpose: that you read what was actually filed.
