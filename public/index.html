Open Primitive
7 primitives live · Why
Open access · No AI summaries · No intermediaries

Public data,

made readable.

Federal agencies publish the record. The DOT tracks every flight delay. The FDA logs every adverse event. CMS scores every hospital. The data has always been public — the problem has been readability.

A CSV with 47 columns isn't usable. An AI summary of it isn't the data. These are direct interfaces to the primary source — built so the record is something you can actually read, search, and act on.
