The rest of the people your tribe used to have.

Every community used to have people who knew things. Which plants healed. Which roads were safe. What to eat through winter. That knowledge passed between people who loved each other. It didn't come with a subscription fee or an ad.

We've lost most of those people. What we have instead is government databases nobody knows how to read — detailed, honest, public data about the things that matter most to your family. Airlines. Cars. Hospitals. Water. Drugs.

Plaintext translates them. No ads. No affiliates. No agenda. Just the knowledge your tribe should have.
