Google set out to organize the world's information and make it universally accessible and useful. Then they discovered advertising. What we have now is a search index optimized for engagement, not accuracy — and AI that synthesizes it all into confident answers with no citations and no accountability.

The public record was never the problem. The DOT publishes every flight delay. NHTSA records every crash. PubMed indexes every published study. CMS tracks every hospital. It was always there.

Nobody made it readable. Open Primitive does. No ads. No affiliates. No agenda. Primary sources. The actual record.
